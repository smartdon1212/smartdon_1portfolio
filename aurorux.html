About Aurorux

Aurorux is a multifunctional, solar-powered device that brings together five powerful features: a fan, lamp, Bluetooth speaker, power bank, and clock. Designed for adventurers, travelers, and areas with limited power supply, Aurorux solves real-life challenges in a compact and elegant way.

My Talk on Aurorux

"Aurorux came from a simple problem — how to survive in places with no light — and I brought these functions together to run on both solar and electricity. It's for camp, students, and anyone who wants power, light."
